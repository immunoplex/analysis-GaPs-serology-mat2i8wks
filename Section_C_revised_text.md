## Note on the two bracketed Stage-2 numbers

The first Phase-10 run reported an arm-only AUC dropping to 0.51 after interval
adjustment, with 54 mothers changing class in a perfectly arm-aligned pattern. That
was an artifact of the residualisation step, which regressed the delivery features on
`ns(w)*arm` and re-centred on the global mean — thereby removing the between-arm
difference (the vaccine effect), not just the interval effect. The corrected step
standardises each mother to her own arm's mean interval, preserving the arm-level
titre. After the fix, expect the classification to be nearly unchanged (high κ,
arm-only AUC ≈ 0.77); use those corrected values in the bracketed fills above. Do
**not** quote the 0.51 value or the interaction-model arm OR (~7×10⁴, a spline-
boundary extrapolation) anywhere.
